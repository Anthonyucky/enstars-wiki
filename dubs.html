The Ensemble Stars! Anime Dub is a dubbed version of the anime, produced and streamed by Funimation. The dub revoices all the character dialogue in English, but doesn't dub the songs. The first 18 episodes is available for free at https://www.funimation.com/en/shows/ensemble-stars/

The Ensemble Dubs!! Bot

The bot features multiple quotes from the dubbed version of the anime, taken directly from the official subtitles with minor adjustments. Quotes are manually chosen and trimmed to fit the character limit, so context may not always be included (which results in some very interesting quotes ).
